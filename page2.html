I'm a problem solver, technology enthusiast, and side projects lover.

I understand what it means to build products that are high-quality, cohesive, and scalable. My goal is to view a product and its features from the eyes of the user and to create an optimized experience that eases pain points and brings joy to the user. I believe it is important to humanize technology by empathizing with the user for a better user experience.

As a former Data Scientist, I have built awareness for the small details, which is essential for working through requirements, visualizations, models, and prototypes with cross-functional team members. I enjoy working with fellow data scientists, engineers, creatives & other business-oriented individuals. I can appropriately approach problem-solving and actively maintain a holistic viewpoint through various phases.

I have a keen interest in product development, product management, entrepreneurship, machine learning, data analytics and visualization . Over the last 3 years, I have taught myself concepts of product development and management and have been involved in multiple projects (and side projects!) ever since. These experiences have given me unique insights into the product lifecycle and has allowed me to think through the entire user journey while challenging my own assumptions.

I'm available to talk about all things product, tech, startups and business. Or, if you just want to connect over a virtual coffee, I'd love that too!
